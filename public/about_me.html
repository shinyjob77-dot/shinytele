Meet Your Provider

Welcome to TeleDNPnow, where compassionate, convenient, and patient-centered healthcare is just a click away.

I am Shiny Job, DNP, FNP-C, a board-certified Family Nurse Practitioner with 14 years of clinical experience caring for patients across a wide range of healthcare settings. My goal is to provide accessible, evidence-based telehealth care while helping patients feel heard, informed, and supported in their healthcare journey.

Education & Training

  • Doctor of Nursing Practice (DNP) - Walden University
  • Family Nurse Practitioner Program - University of Phoenix (Completed in 2011)
  • Board-Certified Family Nurse Practitioner (FNP-C)

Clinical Experience

With over 14 years of experience as a Family Nurse Practitioner, I have cared for patients in diverse healthcare environments, including:

  • Primary Care & Family Practice
  • Chronic Disease Management
  • Urgent & Same-Day Care
  • Correctional Healthcare
  • Tribal & Rural Healthcare / Indian Health Services

My Approach to Care

I believe healthcare should be simple, accessible, compassionate, affordable, and patient-centered. Every patient deserves to feel heard, respected, and supported.

My approach combines evidence-based medicine, patient education, safety, and personalized treatment planning to help patients make informed healthcare decisions. At TeleDNPnow, we are committed to providing affordable, high-quality telehealth care with transparent self-pay options, helping patients access trusted medical care without long waits or unnecessary costs.
