What happens during a lab review?

TeleDNPnow can review recent lab results, explain abnormal values in plain language, discuss follow-up plans, and determine whether additional testing, medication review, lifestyle changes, or referral may be needed.

TeleDNPnow is integrated with major labs including Labcorp and Sonora Quest when available for ordering and review workflows.

Important: Critical lab results or severe symptoms may require urgent or emergency evaluation.
