Medication refill review

TeleDNPnow can review current medications, refill history, side effects, allergies, pharmacy information, and follow-up needs. Some medications require labs, in-person care, specialist management, or additional records before refill.

Controlled substances, narcotics, benzodiazepines, marijuana, and illegal drugs are not provided through TeleDNPnow.

Safety: If your symptoms are severe or worsening, seek urgent or emergency care instead of waiting for a refill visit.
