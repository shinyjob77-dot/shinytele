Common sick visit concerns

TeleDNPnow may help with cold and flu symptoms, sinus symptoms, cough, sore throat questions, mild stomach symptoms, uncomplicated urinary symptoms, minor skin concerns, and medication questions when telehealth is appropriate.

Testing, labs, prescriptions, or in-person evaluation may be recommended depending on symptoms.

Emergency warning: Chest pain, severe breathing trouble, confusion, severe abdominal pain, dehydration, vision loss, severe allergic reaction, or stroke symptoms require emergency care.
