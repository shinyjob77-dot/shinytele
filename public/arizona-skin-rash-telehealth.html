Skin concerns that may fit telehealth

TeleDNPnow may review acne, rash, eczema flares, contact dermatitis, dandruff, itchy skin, folliculitis, mild skin irritation, and other common skin concerns. Clear photos may be requested through a secure method.

Some skin problems require urgent in-person care, labs, culture, or dermatology referral.

Urgent warning: Fever with spreading redness, severe pain, facial swelling, eye involvement, rapidly worsening rash, or signs of serious infection need urgent care.
