Confused About Your Lab Results? Get Clear Answers & Expert Guidance From Home

Had blood work, imaging, or diagnostic testing done and not sure what the results mean? Worried about abnormal labs, cholesterol numbers, diabetes results, thyroid levels, or imaging findings? You should not have to search the internet for answers or wait weeks for clarification. TeleDNPnow is here to help you better understand your health.

At TeleDNPnow, our Lab & Diagnostic Test Review Visits are designed to help you clearly understand your results, discuss what they may mean, and develop a personalized plan for next steps, all from the comfort of your home.

I am Shiny Job, DNP, FNP-C, a Family Nurse Practitioner with 14 years of clinical experience providing evidence-based care for acute and chronic conditions. My goal is to explain your results in simple, understandable language, answer your questions, review symptoms, and help you make informed decisions about your health.

Lab & Diagnostic Review Visits May Include:

  • Blood work review such as CBC, CMP, cholesterol, thyroid, A1C, glucose, vitamin levels, and more
  • Diabetes and cholesterol result review
  • Thyroid and hormone-related lab discussions
  • Urinalysis and common infection-related testing review
  • Imaging review such as X-ray, ultrasound, CT, and MRI reports when appropriate
  • Medication review based on test results
  • Guidance on follow-up care, lifestyle changes, or additional testing

No confusing medical terms. No guessing. Just clear answers and personalized guidance for your health.

Whether your test results are normal, abnormal, or simply confusing, we take the time to review them carefully and discuss what matters most for your health and wellness.

Important Telehealth Disclaimer: Some abnormal lab findings, imaging results, or symptoms may require in-person evaluation, physical examination, additional testing, specialist referral, or urgent onsite care for safe and appropriate management. If needed, you may be advised to follow up with your primary care provider, specialist, urgent care, or emergency services.

With easy scheduling, convenient telehealth visits, and self-pay options available, understanding your health has never been easier.
