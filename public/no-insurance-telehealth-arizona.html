Affordable self-pay virtual care

Healthcare costs and insurance loss can make it difficult to get care. TeleDNPnow provides a convenient self-pay option for non-emergency telehealth visits from home.

Services may include sick visits, medication refill review, lab review, skin concerns, women's health concerns, chronic care support, and weight loss consultation when clinically appropriate.

Emergency warning: Telehealth is not for emergencies. Call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room for severe or life-threatening symptoms.
