Women's Health Made Simple - Compassionate Care From the Comfort of Home

Looking for convenient, supportive women's healthcare without long wait times or crowded clinics? Whether you have questions about birth control, menstrual concerns, hormonal changes, menopause symptoms, vaginal symptoms, wellness, or preventive care, TeleDNPnow is here to support your health journey with compassionate, personalized care.

At TeleDNPnow, our Women's Health Visits are designed to provide convenient, confidential telehealth care tailored to your needs. We understand women's health concerns can feel personal and sometimes overwhelming. Our goal is to make healthcare easier, more comfortable, and accessible from wherever you are.

I am Shiny Job, DNP, FNP-C, a Family Nurse Practitioner with over 14 years of experience providing evidence-based, patient-centered care. I take time to listen to your concerns, answer your questions, and create a care plan focused on your comfort, wellness, and long-term health.

Women's Health Visits May Help With:

  • Birth control counseling and contraceptive refills
  • Menstrual concerns and irregular periods
  • Menopause and hormonal symptom support
  • Vaginal symptoms such as yeast infection, irritation, or discharge concerns when appropriate
  • Urinary symptoms and women's wellness concerns
  • Preventive health guidance and lifestyle support
  • Medication management and routine follow-up care

Private. Convenient. Compassionate Care - Without Leaving Home.

Important Telehealth Disclaimer: Some women's health concerns may require an in-person examination, pelvic exam, laboratory testing, imaging, or urgent onsite evaluation for safe and accurate diagnosis and treatment. If your symptoms require hands-on assessment, you may be advised to follow up with an in-person provider, urgent care, OB/GYN, or emergency care when appropriate.
