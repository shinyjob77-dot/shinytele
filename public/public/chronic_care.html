Managing Chronic Health Conditions Doesn't Have to Be Complicated - Personalized Care From Home

Living with a chronic health condition can feel overwhelming, especially when appointments are hard to schedule, wait times are long, or life gets busy. Whether you are managing high blood pressure, diabetes, asthma, cholesterol, thyroid concerns, or other ongoing health conditions, TeleDNPnow is here to help you stay on track with convenient, compassionate care from the comfort of your home.

At TeleDNPnow, our Chronic Care Visits focus on helping you better manage your health, prevent complications, improve symptoms, and support long-term wellness. We believe chronic care should be proactive, personalized, and easy to access - not stressful.

I am Shiny Job, DNP, FNP-C, a Family Nurse Practitioner with 14 years of clinical experience providing evidence-based care for both acute and chronic conditions. My approach focuses on listening to your concerns, reviewing symptoms, monitoring progress, adjusting treatment plans when appropriate, and helping you feel confident in managing your health.

Chronic Care Visits May Help With:

  • High blood pressure (Hypertension)
  • Diabetes management and medication follow-up
  • High cholesterol (Hyperlipidemia)
  • Asthma and respiratory condition management
  • Thyroid conditions
  • GERD/acid reflux and digestive concerns
  • Medication monitoring and refill management
  • Lifestyle counseling for healthier long-term outcomes

No crowded clinics. No long waits. Just personalized care focused on helping you feel your best.

For safe and effective long-term telehealth chronic care, some patients may need to purchase basic home monitoring equipment when appropriate, including a blood pressure machine, pulse oximeter, thermometer, or blood sugar monitor. These tools can help improve treatment decisions, monitor progress, and support safer, more personalized care from home.

Important Telehealth Disclaimer: Some chronic medical concerns may require in-person evaluation, physical examination, laboratory testing, imaging, specialist referral, or urgent onsite care depending on symptoms and medical needs. If your condition cannot be safely managed through telehealth alone, you may be advised to follow up with an in-person provider or seek urgent/emergency evaluation when appropriate.

With easy scheduling, convenient telehealth visits, and self-pay options available, staying on top of your health has never been easier.
