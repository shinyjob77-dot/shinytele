Need a Prescription Refill? Fast, Easy & Convenient Telehealth Care

Running low on your medication? Can't get in to see your provider soon enough? Need a quick, convenient medication refill without long wait times or unnecessary office visits? TeleDNPnow is here to help.

At TeleDNPnow, we provide Prescription Refill Visits designed to make managing your medications simple, safe, and stress-free, all from the comfort of your home. Whether you need a refill for a chronic condition or medication review, we make the process convenient and personalized.

I am Shiny Job, DNP, FNP-C, a Family Nurse Practitioner with over 14 years of clinical experience providing safe, evidence-based care for acute and chronic conditions. During your refill visit, we review your symptoms, medication effectiveness, side effects, and overall health to ensure your treatment plan continues to work for you.

Common Prescription Refill Visits Include:

  • Blood pressure medications
  • Diabetes medications
  • Cholesterol medications
  • Asthma inhalers and respiratory medications
  • GERD/acid reflux medications
  • Allergy medications
  • Routine non-controlled medication refills

Please Note: TeleDNPnow does not prescribe narcotic pain medications, controlled substances, or chronic opioid medications through telehealth visits. Patient safety and evidence-based care remain our top priorities.

No crowded waiting rooms. No long appointment delays. Just convenient care when you need it most.

With easy scheduling, telehealth convenience, and affordable self-pay options available, getting your medication refill has never been easier.
