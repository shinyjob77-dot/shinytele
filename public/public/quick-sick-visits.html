Telehealth Disclaimer

Disclaimer: TeleDNPnow provides telehealth services for non-emergency medical concerns only. Telehealth has limitations and may not be appropriate for all medical conditions. Some concerns may require in-person evaluation, physical examination, laboratory testing, imaging, specialist referral, or urgent/emergency care. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, chest pain, severe shortness of breath, stroke symptoms, suicidal thoughts, or other life-threatening conditions, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room immediately.

TeleDNPnow does not prescribe narcotic pain medications, controlled substances, chronic opioid therapy, or certain behavioral health medications. Treatment decisions are based on clinical appropriateness, patient safety, and applicable state regulations.

For your privacy and security, personal health information submitted for care is securely managed through CharmHealth EHR, a HIPAA-compliant electronic health record system. TeleDNPnow does not directly store sensitive personal or payment information on the website. Secure payment processing is handled through Bluefin Payment Systems for enhanced payment security and protection.

By using TeleDNPnow services, you acknowledge the limitations of telehealth and understand that medical recommendations may include referral for onsite evaluation or diagnostic testing when clinically necessary.
