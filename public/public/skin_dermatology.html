Concerned About a Rash, Acne, Itching, or Skin Changes? Get Convenient Skin Care From Home

Noticing a new rash, itching, acne flare-up, skin irritation, redness, or another skin concern? Wondering if it is an allergy, infection, eczema, fungal rash, or something that needs medical attention? You do not have to wait weeks for answers. TeleDNPnow is here to help.

At TeleDNPnow, our Skin & Dermatology Visits provide convenient telehealth evaluation for common skin concerns from the comfort and privacy of your home. Using secure telehealth and photo review when appropriate, we help assess symptoms, discuss possible causes, and provide guidance on treatment options and next steps.

I am Shiny Job, DNP, FNP-C, a Family Nurse Practitioner with 14 years of clinical experience providing evidence-based care for a wide variety of acute and chronic health concerns. My goal is to carefully listen to your concerns, review your symptoms, assess skin changes when possible through telehealth, and help you feel confident about your care plan.

Skin & Dermatology Visits May Help With:

  • Rashes and skin irritation
  • Acne and breakouts
  • Eczema and dry skin concerns
  • Allergic skin reactions and itching
  • Fungal skin concerns and athlete's foot
  • Minor skin infections when appropriate
  • Insect bites, redness, or irritation
  • General skin questions and symptom evaluation

Fast Answers. Convenient Care. No Long Wait for Skin Concerns.

Many common skin conditions can be evaluated through telehealth using symptom discussion and clear images when appropriate, helping you receive timely guidance without leaving home.

Important Telehealth Disclaimer: Some skin concerns may require in-person evaluation, diagnostic testing, skin examination, dermoscopy, biopsy, cultures, laboratory testing, imaging, or specialist dermatology referral for safe and accurate diagnosis. Certain conditions, changing moles, severe infections, rapidly worsening rashes, suspicious lesions, or persistent skin changes may require onsite medical evaluation and diagnostic measures.

If your condition cannot be safely diagnosed or treated through telehealth alone, you may be advised to follow up with an in-person provider, dermatologist, urgent care, or emergency services when appropriate.

With easy scheduling, convenient telehealth visits, and self-pay options available, quality skin care support is easier than ever.
