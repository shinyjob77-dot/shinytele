Phoenix telehealth care from home

TeleDNPnow helps Phoenix-area patients access convenient non-emergency telehealth care without sitting in a waiting room. Care is provided by Dr. Shiny Job, DNP, FNP-C, a Family Nurse Practitioner.

Visits may include sick visits, medication refills, lab review, skin and rash concerns, women's health concerns, chronic care follow-up, and weight loss support after a clinical evaluation.

No insurance? No worries.

Self-pay options are available for patients who do not have insurance or prefer direct payment. Insurance options may also be available; please call for current details.

Emergency warning: Telehealth is for non-emergency concerns only. For chest pain, severe shortness of breath, stroke symptoms, severe abdominal pain, vision loss, severe allergic reaction, suicidal thoughts, or any emergency, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room.
