Tucson virtual care options

TeleDNPnow provides telehealth visits for common non-emergency health needs. Patients can discuss symptoms, medication refill needs, lab results, skin concerns, women's health questions, chronic care follow-up, and weight loss options when clinically appropriate.

Care is provided by Dr. Shiny Job, DNP, FNP-C, a Family Nurse Practitioner serving Arizona patients by telehealth.

Affordable access

Self-pay visits are available, and insurance options may be available. Call TeleDNPnow for current information before booking if you have insurance questions.

Emergency warning: For medical emergencies, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room. TeleDNPnow does not replace urgent in-person care when needed.
