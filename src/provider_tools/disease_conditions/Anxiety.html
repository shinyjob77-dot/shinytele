Anxiety Differential Diagnosis

Condition Signs & Symptoms Medications Treatment Cost of Meds Insurance Labs / Diagnostics Precautions
Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD) Excessive worry >=6 months, fatigue, irritability, muscle tension, poor sleep Sertraline, escitalopram, venlafaxine, buspirone CBT + SSRI/SNRI first-line $10-50/month (generic) Usually covered; therapy may need authorization TSH, CBC, CMP if indicated Monitor for SSRI activation, suicidality in young patients
Panic Disorder Recurrent panic attacks, palpitations, chest pain, fear of dying Sertraline, paroxetine, venlafaxine, short-term benzodiazepines CBT + SSRI/SNRI $10-150/month Covered; benzos restricted ECG, TSH, consider cardiac workup Rule out cardiac disease; avoid long-term benzodiazepines
Social Anxiety Disorder Fear of social situations, avoidance, performance anxiety Sertraline, paroxetine, venlafaxine CBT (exposure) + SSRI $10-50/month Usually covered Clinical diagnosis Avoid alcohol self-medication
Specific Phobia Fear of specific object/situation, immediate anxiety response Usually none Exposure therapy N/A Therapy may be limited None Avoid unnecessary medication use
PTSD Flashbacks, nightmares, hypervigilance, avoidance Sertraline, paroxetine Trauma-focused CBT, EMDR $10-50/month Therapy may require referral Clinical diagnosis Assess suicide risk
OCD Obsessions and compulsions, intrusive thoughts Fluoxetine, sertraline, clomipramine CBT (ERP) + high-dose SSRI $10-100/month Covered; specialty therapy limited Clinical diagnosis Requires higher SSRI doses
Substance-Induced Anxiety Related to caffeine, stimulants, alcohol withdrawal Treat underlying cause Substance cessation, detox if needed Variable Detox programs vary Urine drug screen Avoid benzodiazepines in SUD
Medical Condition (e.g., Hyperthyroidism) Palpitations, weight loss, tremor, heat intolerance Treat underlying condition Medical management of cause Variable Depends on condition TSH, T4, ECG, metanephrines if indicated Do not miss organic etiology
Depression with Anxiety Low mood, anhedonia, anxiety symptoms SSRIs, SNRIs Treat depression (CBT + meds) $10-50/month Covered PHQ-9, clinical assessment Monitor suicidality
