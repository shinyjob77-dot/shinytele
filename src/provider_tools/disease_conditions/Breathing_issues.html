Emergency Red Flags - Immediate ER Referral

  • Severe respiratory distress
  • Oxygen saturation below 92%
  • Cyanosis or blue lips
  • Unable to speak full sentences
  • Severe chest pain
  • Altered mental status or confusion
  • Rapid worsening symptoms despite inhaler use
Clinical disclaimer: This table is for professional education and clinical decision-support only. It does not replace clinician judgment, local protocols, or emergency evaluation when indicated. Medication choices and dosing should be individualized based on age, pregnancy status, allergies, comorbidities, renal/hepatic function, severity, and current guidelines.
